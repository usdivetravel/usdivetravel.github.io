  • There are no triples booked into Villa at Dunbar Rock / Nautilus Dive Resort.

  • $12 per room per day for use of air conditioner, since electricity is Diesel-generated & therefore quite costly on remote Guanaja, a pristine island about 40 miles from the Honduras mainland.

  • Nautilus Dive Resort also charges all clients, all seasons a $10 per guest tariff to cover the costly long-distance phone calls to Atlantido or Sosa Airlines in San Pedro Sula, mainland Honduras. This is how we secure your booking of commuter airline tickets from San Pedro to Guanaja Island, since the little carrier does not operate on the international computer reservation system.

  • The St. Paul, MN office of U.S. Dive Travel is now, by special agreement, the exclusive North American representative for Villa at Dunbar Rock / Nautilus Dive Resort, the value destination for clients who seek Guanaja diving. Therefore all clients from the U.S. or Canada who wish to book a dive package at Villa at Dunbar Rock / Nautilus Resort are asked to phone or e-mail the U.S. Dive Travel office at 952-953-4124; or e-mail us at: divetrip@bitstream.net.

  • Round-trip commuter air tickets from San Pedro Sula to Guanaja are secured for you by U.S. Dive Travel's reservation staff here stateside. Normally you do not need to pay for your Sosa tickets before departure. That's because U.S. Dive Travel & Dunbar Villa / Nautilus Dive Resort will reserve your Sosa seats via phone calls to Honduras well before you leave. USDT will give you a voucher, which is carefully checked & double-checked for accuracy. This guarantees you a seat on the commuter flight. You take that voucher with you when you leave the U.S. then present it to the airline ticket counter at SAP Airport in San Pedro Sula. Your tickets will have been pre-paid. Then clients pay for their actual tickets with U.S. dollars, upon arrival at Villa at Dunbar Rock / Nautilus Resort. After this begins the Honduras diving fun, your Guanaja diving adventure!

    The Dunbar Villa / Nautilus Resort package includes: 7 nights lodging in a quiet, immaculate 3-star inn + 5 days of 3-tank boat diving + 1 day of 2-tank boat diving (last day) + 3 superb home-cooked meals per day + 1 free night dive per week (weather & currents permitting) + round-trip airport transfers on Guanaja (pronounced Gwa-NAH-hah) + all hotel taxes pre-paid. Tips are extra. All beverages & water-taxi transportation for personal side tours also are extra costs. The gracious staff at Villa at Dunbar Rock / Nautilus Dive Resort essentially becomes your private Domestic Dream Team for a Honduras diving week. You will have your own personal chefs, maids, gardeners, divemasters & boat tenders. This is the way to do Honduras diving in the Bay Islands. It's a treat to find dive resorts like this, where service is still the priority. This is a great way to enjoy your Honduras dive trip.
