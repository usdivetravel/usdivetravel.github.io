Ocean Club & Ocean Club West are both nestled along the quiet shores of Grace Bay, one of the Caribbean’s last hidden treasures. The beaches are like talcum powder. The waters are clear & warm, with more hues of turquoise blue & emerald green than you’ve ever seen. The sun always seems to be shining. & on most days, you can even expect a visit from Jo-Jo, a wild dolphin who's come to call Ocean Club home.

Casually elegant suites surround a divine tropical oasis. An Azure sea, pristine white sand & a palette of pastels await you within our tropical gardens. Resplendent with a myriad of colorful flowers & swaying palms. Ocean Club has a welcoming allure in each one of its 86 suites overlooking a stretch of 12-mile long Grace Bay Beach. At Ocean Club West, land & sea, relaxation & recreation, grand style & simple elegance are in perfect harmony.

Both Ocean Club & Ocean Club West are surrounded by lush tropical gardens, Caribbean freeform pools and, of course, a breath-taking beach. Here you can do it all or nothing at all. Enjoy onsite water sports, scuba diving, or swimming in one of our freshwater pools. Play tennis, work out in our fitness center or wind down with a soothing massage. After all, one needs to leave some comfy white space in all Provo scuba diving & snorkeling packages -- just to unwind & let the old city life peel away! That's the name of the island game here at both Ocean Club Resort & Ocean Club West.

Nearby a variety of activities awaits, inviting you to delight in a boating excursion, sport fishing or a challenging game of golf across the street at the Provo Golf Club. Or you may just want to take part in Ocean Club's most popular activity of all, doing absolutely nothing. Bask on our sun-drenched beach or wade into our warm, crystal-clear turquoise sea. The sea is like a living gem here, & is the heart & soul of our Provo scuba diving & snorkeling packages.

Dine at one of the best restaurants in the Turks & Caicos Islands, Ocean Club's own Gecko Grille. It specializes in the artful blending of fresh Caribbean seafood & the finest continental cuts of meat, grilled to perfection, with zesty island spices. For casual dinning, we offer the oceanfront Cabana Bar & Grille, open to any guest at either Ocean Club Resort & Ocean Club West.

Whether you want to do a lot of activities or just "veg in the sun", you'll find we have created an an intimate hideaway where the prevailing mood is relaxed sophistication. Ocean Club & Ocean Club West are the cat's pajamas in 5-star tropical island getaways, & our certain best pick for top-quality Turks & Caicos diving & snorkeling packages.
