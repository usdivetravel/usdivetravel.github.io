A warm welcome as you visit our new Internet Hub, celebrating a spirited island nation that captivates hearts & inspires memories forever -- the friendly FIJI ISLANDS of the SOUTH PACIFIC !

 

Are these sun-blessed, ecologically upre isles perhaps the most hospitable culture anywhere in the South Pacific?

 

Are Fiji's magnificent coral reefs & sugar-sand beaches among the finest maritime vacation getaways on our stressed-out, post-modern planet?

 

Yes, we believe so, & with informed certainty.  And that's why our thoughts drift back to Fiji vacations almost daily, with a bracing sense of nostalgia, like missing your kid who just left home for college, because …



The FIJI ISLANDS are the only island nation among dozens we have visited these last 3+ decades of adventure tours, that always inspires the same heartfelt response each time we prep to leave our final day.

 

Without fail, it happens every time … we fight back profound sadness. We push it away & keep on smiling so our hosts will feel blessed & honored. Almost feels a little lame to admit this, as if we should be more thick-skinned, more jaded & more "all-business" after decades of leading adventure tours on 5 continents.

 

But it happens. Fiji makes it happen every time. And why is that, sunseekers?

 

This sudden feeling wells up from a very deep place in psyches normally calm & matter-of-fact about beautiful islands. After all, tropical islands are our work. This bittersweet emotion carries two separate soul vectors at once: sadness that it may be years before we get to enjoy the people, the beaches & the magical reefs we have come to love; & gratitude that we were blessed enough to experience these sweet sensations once again -- in a world so troubled with traffic, turmoil, noise & nonsense.

 

Fiji vacations have an amazing power, for a week or two, to sweep away all the stress & static that pass for living in an urban culture of the USA, or in metro Canada, Japan or Europe. And A Fiji vacation will replace that G7 urban stress with gee-whiz joys, almost a childlike re-energizing of the spirit. It's wonderful, compelling & once it gets in your blood, Fiji never lets you go!

 

For us, & for hundreds of our clients since the early 1990s, Fiji has become a uniquely powerful place of healing, humor, refreshment & relaxation. So many folks have phoned us or written letters after their return, thanking us for sending them to Fiji, describing the same unexpected flow of emotion we've felt upon leaving Fiji, we have lost count now.

 

We call it "The Fiji Phenomenon."

 

Fiji diving & Fiji snorkeling grab you, right where you live! And now we'll move along, tropical travelers, back to the nitty-gritty of getting there, the fun dynamics of planning carefully, cost-efficiently, for the adventure vacation of a lifetime …

Enough of the nuts & bolts of tropical travel.  Fiji is about spirit, & here is why my wife Susan & I feel such a strong bond with the FIJI ISLANDS, & why we always feel intrigued about the FIJI ISLANDS & their charms.

The people of Fiji are out-of-this-world gracious, & hospitable to a degree seldom encountered anywhere in our frantic MTV-flogged American culture.  Fijian people smile & they light up your room.  They are uncommonly  kind & refreshing people to deal with.  We have found that time passes quickly amongst people this spiritually gifted & serene.

One more info tidbit....did you know, as part of their traditional culture, Fijians will not even accept tips from visitors on those outer islands, because their village social norms compel them to warm service as a way of life, not a means to cajole tips.  They actually love to serve & have honed pure service to an art form.  Without any ostentatious show of self-righteousness.  Just quietly & effectively.

The service on our Fijian live-aboards & our island resorts is exceptional & never snooty or aloof as in French Polynesia.

Fiji really grabbed us by the heart more than 20 years ago & never let go.  We can hardly wait to go back, soon, & perhaps even take our two boys when the timing is apropos.  To wit, Susan & I have spent, collectively, more than 90 weeks touring, diving & photographing numerous islands across Polynesia since the late 1980s, & we find Fiji to be -- far & away -- our favorite SoPac destination for these reasons:

* Fiji offers the friendliest & most un-self-conscious indigenous people we have been blessed to work with anywhere in the Pacific. They are kind, soft-spoken & hospitable to a fault. Almost makes you feel humbled by their gentle nature & demure attitudes. They are delightful people! Within 30 minutes of your first day in Fiji, you will know what we mean.

* Topside visual delights from mountain vistas to towering waterfalls, to fun caverns to colorful foliage & birds of exotic hues plus flowering trees of every imaginable shape & color. Fiji is a garden island nation that offers countless little fun discoveries every day to the discerning eye. We love being there.  Our clients write us e-mails often, even fun old-fashioned postcards sometimes the second day after they arrive, brimming with enthusiasm about their new adventure.

* You must see these stunning underwater vistas, the profusion of soft corals that sometimes can be among the largest gorgonia & soft colonies in the SoPac. Large pelagics from reef sharks & rays to schools of small psychedelic-hued fish that come across your path by the hundreds sometimes. The Somosomo Straits between Taveuni & Vanua Levu offer some of the best coral reef drift diving in the world. The shallow channels of Beqa Lagoon, Taveuni, Kadavu & Matangi island area are world-class reef environments, just brimming with colors, & up to 300 species of fish, even more coral varieties.
