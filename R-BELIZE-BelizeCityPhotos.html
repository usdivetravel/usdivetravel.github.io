Our tropical vacation experts normally secure wholesale discount air tickets for our clients who book early enough to secure limited seats in the best price categories. We can & WILL hold the line on Belize scuba diving & Belize snorkeling packages, because those contracts are secured one year in advance; & when you book early enough we secure wholesale air tickets.

At our Belize dive resorts, seldom will triple-occupancy rooms be offered -- & normally only to parents with young children. International & commuter "island-hopper" airfares are always extra above these land costs. Nominal service fees are also extra for air tickets & the vessel + side tour components. The baseline tariffs for all clients on our Belize dive vacations start at $55 per person for the land portion + $35 pp for the air tickets. Late-booking clients on all our Belize scuba diving & Belize snorkeling packages may receive slightly higher tariffs on the lodging + diving at many of our dive resorts. Solo clients on most of our Belize scuba diving & Belize snorkeling packages normally will pay a single supplement to secure a private room -- typically about 30% more.

For all our Belize scuba diving & Belize snorkeling packages the preferred payment mode for dive resorts & side tours is by e-check, ACH or wire transfer in U.S. dollars. No personal checks will be accepted for the land portion of any Belize diving vacation reservation. Thank you for your gracious understanding. Our service level is the highest & our prices the lowest in this industry, & thus we need to preserve a reasonable margin. For ultra-discount wholesale air tickets, USDT accepts only cashier's checks or wire transfers, please, for all our Belize dive vacations.
